About

For over 20 years, the Unicorn Project has been working to bring awareness to the endangerment and near extinction of unicorns. Hunted for their horns and the magical properties of their hair and blood, unicorns are highly prized on the black market. The mass modernization and deforestation around the world has also proven problematic to the unicorn population. This is why we at the Unicorn Project have been campaigning for the legal protection of unicorns, as well as going into the field to rescue unicorns from dangerous situations. Our overall goal is to provide unicorns with safe environments in which they can prosper in peace and continue to bring magic and beauty to the world.
